THE OHIO STATE UNIVERSITY
WEXNER MEDICAL CENTER

Increase the number of patients from outside central Ohio.

The Ohio State University Wexner Medical Center ranks among the nation’s best treatment facilities. Most Ohioans are aware of this, but on a national scale, this was not the case. So, we set out to educate the nation.

Result

Using the Lift platform, the campaign quickly made a widespread impact that continues to grow today.

Brand Advocacy

11.3%

TOTAL ADVERTISING RECALL

18.6%

Most importantly, more patients are traveling to The Ohio State University and are eager to share their stories to sustain the momentum and help educate the nation.

EXECUTION

The multi-year integrated campaign featured patients from counties outside the central Ohio area explaining why they chose Ohio State’s Wexner Medical Center for their medical care. Campaign channels included print, digital and TV/web video.

Print

THE CAMPAIGN STRETCHES ACROSS TELEVISION, WEB AND PRINT. THE OBJECTIVE WAS TO INCREASE AWARENESS SO AUDIENCES WOULD ASK THEIR DOCTORS ABOUT THE CENTER AND THEREBY INCREASE THE NUMBER OF PATIENTS VISITING FROM OUTSIDE CENTRAL OHIO.
